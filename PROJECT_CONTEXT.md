# JEE Study Buddy

## Vision

JEE Study Buddy is an AI-powered mentor that helps students prepare for JEE by teaching concepts, solving doubts, generating quizzes, tracking progress, and creating personalized study plans.

The AI remembers each student's weak topics and continuously adapts its teaching strategy.

- JEE Advanced
- Class 11
- Class 12

## Core Features

1. 🤖 AI Teacher

Explains concepts in multiple difficulty levels.

---

2. 📸 Doubt Solver

Accepts text and image questions.

---

3. 📝 AI Quiz Generator

Creates quizzes from selected topics.

---

4. 📅 Personalized Study Planner

Generates daily study plans.

---

5. 📊 Progress Dashboard

Visualizes strengths and weaknesses.

---

6. 🧠 Weak Topic Memory

Stores concepts where students repeatedly struggle.

---

7. 🎯 Adaptive AI Coach

Adjusts teaching style and encouragement based on student performance.

---

8. 🔄 Smart Revision

Schedules revision using previous mistakes and study history.
